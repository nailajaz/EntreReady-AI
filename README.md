# 🚀 EntreReady AI (ERAI)

## An Explainable AI Decision Support Framework for Assessing Entrepreneurial Readiness, Identifying Development Gaps, and Providing Personalized Recommendations

---

## Overview

EntreReady AI (ERAI) is an AI-enabled decision support system designed to assess entrepreneurial readiness by evaluating multiple dimensions associated with successful venture creation.

The prototype provides an explainable readiness assessment, identifies individual development gaps, and generates personalized recommendations to support aspiring entrepreneurs, educators, incubators, and entrepreneurship development programs.

ERAI has been developed as part of an ongoing academic research project integrating entrepreneurship, artificial intelligence, and explainable decision support systems.

---

## Key Features

- Entrepreneurial Readiness Assessment
- Multi-Dimensional Evaluation Framework
- Explainable AI Assessment Logic
- Personalized Development Recommendations
- Interactive Dashboard
- Radar Chart Visualization
- Readiness Classification
- CSV Export of Assessment Results

---

## Assessment Dimensions

The current prototype evaluates entrepreneurial readiness across six dimensions:

1. Entrepreneurial Motivation
2. Entrepreneurial Self-Efficacy
3. Innovation & Opportunity Recognition
4. Risk Management & Resilience
5. Business & AI Readiness
6. Resources & Support

---

## Readiness Output

The system generates:

- ERAI Readiness Index (0–100)
- Readiness Classification
- Dimension Scores
- Entrepreneurial Strengths
- Development Gaps
- Personalized Recommendations

## Research Background

This prototype operationalizes an entrepreneurial readiness framework developed through academic research in entrepreneurship and artificial intelligence.

The prototype is designed to demonstrate how AI-enabled decision support systems can translate validated entrepreneurial constructs into practical assessment and development tools.
